What We Do

Racing, Simulating,
and Building.

IUTAUS currently drives three active projects that put students on real racing grids, international sim platforms, and the EV engineering frontier.

Formula IUT

IUT's FSAE team competing in Formula Student China 2025 and multiple international events — earning podium finishes and representing Bangladesh on the global motorsport stage.

Red Arrows Sim Racing

A sim racing and race engineering programme teaching students racecraft and telemetry. Competing as Formula IUT Esports, the team qualified for FSUK 2026 at Williams — one of the most prestigious virtual racing stages.

Blitzcart

An electric go-kart built in-house by Formula IUT — currently under active manufacturing. Blitzcart is IUTAUS's push into EV engineering, designed and assembled entirely by students from the ground up.

Annual Publication

Engine Insider

Engine Insider is the official annual magazine of the IUT Automobile Society — a curated collection of student articles, technical writeups, project retrospectives, and automotive perspectives from the minds at IUTAUS. Each edition captures the year's milestones, the lessons learned on and off the track, and the stories that drive us forward.
